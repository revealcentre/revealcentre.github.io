Using Virtual Reality to Simulate Street Harassment
Reward: £10 Amazon voucher for ~1 hour
In this study you will be able to try out modern VR hardware and experience a VR simulation of verbal street harassment. You will be asked to complete questionnaires and an interview about the experience. During the study, heart rate and skin conductivity will be measured to gain insight into how people respond to such VR experiences. This study has received ethical approval from the Psychology REsearch Ethics Committee (PREC) reference 21-192.
Eligibility: 18 years old or above, have normal or corrected normal vision, have normal or corrected normal hearing, don't suffer from or have a family history of epilepsy.
Location: Human-Computer Interaction Lab at 1 West 2.58

Skyrim in Virtual Reality
Reward: £30 Amazon voucher for 8 sessions of VR play and qestionnaires (less than 1 hour each) on 8 separate days
In this study you get to play Skyrim in VR, using modern VR hardware, for ~30min per day over 8 days (not necessarily consecutive). Immerse yourself into the beautiful world of Skyrim and at the same time, help us research ways of moving in VR by answering some questionnaires about your experience.
Eligibility: 18 years old or above, have normal or corrected normal vision, have normal or corrected normal hearing, don't suffer from or have a family history of epilepsy.
Location: Human-Computer Interaction Lab at 1 West 2.58
